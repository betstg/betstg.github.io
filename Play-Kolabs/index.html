RPGs where every character has a brain

An open-source AI Game Master platform — in the spirit of AI Dungeon and Fables.gg — where characters don't just react. They remember, feel, and keep living their own lives.

Explore a 3D brain View on GitHub

A real brain per character

Memories are filed where biology stores them — hippocampus, cortex, prefrontal cortex, amygdala.

Hormones & body

Dopamine, cortisol, oxytocin, adrenaline — plus blood oxygen, heart rate, and glucose that shape mood and choices.

They live on their own

Characters eat, rest, train, plan, and scheme in the background — even when nobody is playing.

Associative memory

A neural graph where recall blends recency, importance, and relevance, and links grow stronger with use.

AI Game Master

Narrates from the world's lore and each character's actual recalled memories, powered by Gemini.

Open source & free to self-host

MIT-licensed engine. Run it on your own computer at no cost.

A character's mind, in 3D

Each sphere is a memory, fixed in the brain region that stores it. Lines are associations. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom.
